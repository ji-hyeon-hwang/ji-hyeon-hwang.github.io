Jihyeon Hwang

Undergraduate Researcher · UNIST, Electrical Engineering

Hello! I am an undergraduate student in Electrical Engineering at UNIST, working in the Intelligent Systems Lab (ISL) under Prof. Kyuho Lee.

My research focuses on building efficient hardware accelerators and HW-SW co-designed systems for real-time AI workloads — particularly in autonomous driving perception. I am broadly interested in Systems for ML, AI Algorithm-System Co-design, and HW-SW Acceleration.

Recent projects include a real-time 3D LiDAR point cloud segmentation system and a 28nm ASIC accelerator for bird's-eye-view semantic segmentation in multi-camera systems.
