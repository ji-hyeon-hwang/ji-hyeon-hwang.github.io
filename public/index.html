Hello, my name is Jihyeon Hwang. I’m a senior undergraduate student at Ulsan National Institute of Science and Technology (UNIST).

During my undergraduate years, I had the privilege of working with Prof. Kyuho Lee and wonderful team Intelligent Systems Lab (ISL). My research focused on hardware accelerator design and HW-SW co-design for automotive driving and computer vision.

Also I am an AI Engineer at Muscle SYNC, a startup developing a sensorless muscle activation analysis service based on computer vision AI. Founded with teammates I met at a hackathon, we are building an innovative product that redefines how muscle engagement is analyzed — without the need for physical sensors. My research in digital SoC was driven by the need I experienced while developing products at Muscle SYNC — the need for seamless hardware-software optimization.

Nowadays, I’ve been working on LLM agent research and human avatar modeling. These projects reflect my broader goal of being engineer that can advantage human through technologies.

I am always happy to chat about various research fields. Feel free to reach out to collaborate!
